Visual Residues

The following videos document visual residue phenomena captured in different Echo Zones. These visual fragments recur under specific magnetic field conditions, typically associated with intense emotional events.

Silent Care

The daughter quietly tidies the house, restoring order to the space her father has left in grief. Her actions are small, but they speak louder than words—an attempt to keep the family’s memory alive, even when silence fills the home.

Waves and Echoes

An empty shoreline stretches before the camera. The waves repeat endlessly, like echoes returning from the past. The absence of people turns the sea into a mirror of memory—vast, distant, and ungraspable.

The Day She Left

In the dim silence of their home, Ada’s life slips away. The hand Ethan clutches falls, and his desperate cry—“Ada, please…”—echoes unanswered. In the days that follow, he wanders through deserted streets, haunted by the memory of her final moment. The vision of her departure lingers like an echo he cannot escape.

Through the Screen

Ethan stumbles upon the Echo Zone. Through the lens of his phone, a faint image of his late wife appears. The world around him is empty, yet the screen preserves traces of what is gone. He stares in disbelief, caught between grief and wonder.

Aftermath

The camera drifts along his lifeless form. He opens his eyes, hollow, as if falling into an abyss. The bottle at his side is all he clings to—he drinks, each swallow bitter, each sip meant to numb. Yet the emptiness swells, silence roaring inside him. He drowns in echoes of his own making.
