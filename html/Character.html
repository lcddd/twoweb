Character Profiles

Case #EZ-2023-07

In a near-future world affected by magnetic instability, specific zones known as "echo fields" began to appear. Within these spaces, fragments of sound and light from the past loop and repeat. This case file documents the residents of Echo Zone Site #17, a suburban home where anomalous acoustic phenomena were first reported in 2015.

Echo Zone Phenomenon

The government has issued strict warnings: no overnight stays are allowed. During sleep, the human mind is at its most relaxed, and those who fall asleep inside risk losing the ability to distinguish memory from reality.

Ethan Lin

Father / 40s / Maintenance Worker

A quiet maintenance worker who has been emotionally distant since the sudden death of his wife. He lives apart from his daughter and has buried his grief deep inside. Haunted by the fact that he never heard his wife's final words, he becomes obsessed with echo fields, convinced they might hold the closure he desperately needs.

Wife

Deceased / Voice Echo

Ethan's wife who passed away suddenly. Her final words remain unknown to Ethan, creating a void in his life that he desperately tries to fill through echo fields. Her voice echoes in the magnetic fields, repeating fragmented phrases that Ethan obsessively tries to piece together.

Ada

Teenager / 20 years old

Ethan's teenage daughter who lives apart from him following her mother's death. She struggles with loneliness and the emotional distance of her father, who has become increasingly consumed by his obsession with echo fields. Her quiet pleas for connection go unheard as Ethan retreats further into his grief.

Case Resolution

After months of obsession with the echo field, Ethan finally reaches a turning point. One night, he overhears his daughter expressing her loneliness and fears. The next day, he returns to the echo zone one last time, not to cling to the past, but to finally let go.

"He steps out of the echo zone, and as he steps across the boundary, a small, quiet smile appears on his face."

Ethan begins the slow process of reconnecting with his daughter, finally acknowledging the pain they've both been carrying and taking the first steps toward healing.
